### Tracking Tech & Cookies (Digital Breadcrumbs!)
Alright, so we use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and to store some specific info. These tracking goodies include beacons, tags, and scripts, all working together to collect and track data, helping us spruce up and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies:** So, a Cookie is just a tiny file that gets placed on your device. You can totally tell your browser to ditch all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But here's the catch: if you don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not play nice. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say 'no thanks' to Cookies, our Service might just be using them. Just a heads up! 🍪
- **Web Beacons:** Picture these as tiny electronic files (also called clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs) that might be embedded in some parts of our Service or even our emails. These little guys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited certain pages or opened an email. They're also used for other cool website stats, like tracking how popular a section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good. Pretty neat, right? ✨

BTW, Cookies can be either 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. Persistent ones stick around on your computer or mobile device even after you've gone offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof! 💨

We actually use both Session and Persistent Cookies for a few specific reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type:** Session Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are super important for getting you the services available through the Website and letting you use all its cool features. They help us verify users and keep fraudulent account use at bay. Seriously, without these, the services you ask for just wouldn't be possible, and we only use them to give you those services. They're the unsung heroes! 🦸

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These little guys are all about remembering if you've already given a nod to our use of Cookies on the Website. One less thing for you to worry about! ✅

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are like your personal assistant, enabling us to recall the choices you make when you're using the Website – things like remembering your login deets or language preferences. The whole point is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet, right? 🌟

Wanna dig deeper into the Cookies we use and your choices about 'em? Just hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy. All the deets are there! 📖
